Abstract
 The Department of Energy has created a timeline of the history and making of electric vehicles. The site includes chronological time periods, running you through areas that implimented the use of electric vehicles, as well as the inventors that helped improve vehicles batteries, circuits, ect. It introduces the vehicles as early as 1828 and explains their future as recent as 2015. Within this timeline, information of milestones such as Toyota's first hyrbid vehicle in 1997, and Silicon Valley's start up on Tesla Motors can be found.
